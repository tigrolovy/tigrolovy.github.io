I did not have to invent anything. Life was crowded in my soul and escaped like Niagara.
I loved the country I wrote about as my second homeland, though I fell into it as a slave ... "- said the author

"I did not just write, I'm alive!
And he was filled with the life repeated with such a terrible force that exceeded the power of reality for many times. "
